MS. MAUSBERG

BEHIND THE CONCEPT

The Ms. Mausberg situation is a symbolism of today's generational drugs: sex, money, drugs, alcohol, fashion and murder. [Unless you can name the rest.] But really, the second-guessing keeps going. A young woman so estranged, and nobody knows what world she's living in. (Nor will they care.) No real/legal nine to five or nothing, just hustlin' back to back. She's thick, curvy, long hair, VVS colored eyes, nails super accurate, VVS everything [from her ears, to her neck, to her wrist, and her belly button]. Her relationship with her family is a playground seasaw when it comes to growth. She cannot sunseek from her own world full of invasions of troubles, without certain people trying to help her realize she's in too deep in the game. Peers think she doesn't know that the game she's caught up in and attempting to soak up a lot, can later be a setup. But however, she's not really into simple romance nor everybody's full blown lecture/sermon. She has her own ways to get by. How much you wanna bet she's crazier than a gold digger? If she thinks bands can make her dance her way out of her bad karma, is a that gonna magically happen to her in real life? Would say that to her with your chest out or let or slide? But she's not expecting the streets to put her in a box in a certain way. - Anthony Louis Johnson
